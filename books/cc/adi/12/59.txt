====== Cc. Ādi 12.59 ======

===== Text =====

> nandinī, āra kāmadeva, caitanya-dāsa
> durlabha viśvāsa, āra vanamāli-dāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nandinī]]---of the name Nandinī; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:k:kāmadeva]]---of the name Kāmadeva; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Caitanya dāsa; [[synonyms:d:durlabha]] [[synonyms:v:viśvāsa]]---of the name Durlabha Viśvāsa; [[synonyms:ā:āra]]---and; [[synonyms:v:vanamāli]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---of the name Vanamālī dāsa.

===== Translation =====

**Nandinī, Kāmadeva, Caitanya dāsa, Durlabha Viśvāsa and Vanamālī dāsa were the tenth, eleventh, twelfth, thirteenth and fourteenth branches of Śrī Advaita Ācārya.**
