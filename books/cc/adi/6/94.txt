====== Cc. Ādi 6.94 ======

===== Text =====

> jala-tulasī diyā kare kāyāte sevana
> bhakti pracāriyā saba tārilā bhuvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jala]]-[[synonyms:t:tulasī]]---Ganges water and //tulasī// leaves; [[synonyms:d:diyā]]---offering together; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---does; [[synonyms:k:kāyāte]]---with the body; [[synonyms:s:sevana]]---worship; [[synonyms:b:bhakti]]---the cult of devotional service; [[synonyms:p:pracāriyā]]---preaching; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:t:tārilā]]---delivered; [[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---the universe.

===== Translation =====

**With His body He worshiped the Lord by offering Ganges water and tulasī leaves, and by preaching devotional service He delivered the entire universe.**
