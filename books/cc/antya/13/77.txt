====== Cc. Antya 13.77 ======

===== Text =====

> jagadānandera āgamane sabāra ullāsa
> ei-mate nīlācale prabhura vilāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jagadānandera]]---of Jagadānanda Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:ā:āgamane]]---upon the return; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]] [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---everyone was jubilant; [[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mate]]---in this way; [[synonyms:n:nīlācale]]---at Jagannātha Purī; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:vilāsa]]---pastime.

===== Translation =====

**When Jagadānanda Paṇḍita returned from Vṛndāvana, everyone was jubilant. Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu enjoyed His pastimes while residing at Jagannātha Purī.**
