====== Cc. Antya 14.15 ======

===== Text =====

> divyonmāde aiche haya, ki ihā vismaya?
> adhirūḍha-bhāve divyonmāda-pralāpa haya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāde]]---in transcendental madness; [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:k:ki]] [[synonyms:i:ihā]] [[synonyms:v:vismaya]]---what is the wonder; [[synonyms:a:adhirūḍha]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāve]]---in highly elevated love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:d:divya]]-[[synonyms:u:unmāda]]---in transcendental madness; [[synonyms:p:pralāpa]]---talking; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---there is.

===== Translation =====

**Such is the state of transcendental madness. Why is it difficult to understand? When one is highly elevated in love of Kṛṣṇa, he becomes transcendentally mad and talks like a madman.**
