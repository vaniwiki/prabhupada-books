====== Cc. Antya 14.57 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata ardha-rātri kailā niryāpaṇa
> bhitara-prakoṣṭhe prabhure karāilā śayana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:a:ardha]]-[[synonyms:r:rātri]]---half the night; [[synonyms:k:kailā]] [[synonyms:n:niryāpaṇa]]---passed; [[synonyms:b:bhitara]]-[[synonyms:p:prakoṣṭhe]]---in the inner room; [[synonyms:p:prabhure]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:karāilā]] [[synonyms:ś:śayana]]---they made to lie down.

===== Translation =====

**After half the night had passed in this way, Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī made Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lie down on His bed in the inner room.**
