====== Cc. Antya 16.89 ======

===== Text =====

> bhoga sarile jagannāthera sevaka-gaṇa
> prasāda lañā prabhu-ṭhāñi kaila āgamana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhoga]] [[synonyms:s:sarile]]---when the food was taken away; [[synonyms:j:jagannāthera]]---of Lord Jagannātha; [[synonyms:s:sevaka]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---the servants; [[synonyms:p:prasāda]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking the //prasāda//; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]-[[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:k:kaila]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamana]]---came.

===== Translation =====

**When the ārati finished, the prasāda was taken out, and the servants of Lord Jagannātha came to offer some to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.**
