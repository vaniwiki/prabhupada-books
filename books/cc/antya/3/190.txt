====== Cc. Antya 3.190 ======

===== Text =====

> 'gopāla cakravartī' nāma eka-jana
> majumadārera ghare sei ārindā pradhāna

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gopāla]] [[synonyms:c:cakravartī]]---Gopāla Cakravartī; [[synonyms:n:nāma]]---named; [[synonyms:e:eka]]-[[synonyms:j:jana]]---one person; [[synonyms:m:majumadārera]] [[synonyms:g:ghare]]---at the residence of Hiraṇya and Govardhana Majumadāra; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---he; [[synonyms:ā:ārindā]] [[synonyms:p:pradhāna]]---the chief tax collector.

===== Translation =====

**At the house of Hiraṇya and Govardhana Majumadāra, a person named Gopāla Cakravartī was officially the chief tax collector.**
