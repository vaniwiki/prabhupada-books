====== Cc. Antya 3.44 ======

===== Text =====

> dāmodara āge svātantrya nā haya kāhāra
> tāra bhaye sabe kare saṅkoca vyavahāra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:d:dāmodara]] [[synonyms:ā:āge]]---in front of Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:s:svātantrya]]---independent behavior; [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:h:haya]] [[synonyms:k:kāhāra]]---no one dares to do; [[synonyms:t:tārabhaye]]---due to fear of him; [[synonyms:s:sabe]]---all of them; [[synonyms:k:kare]]---do; [[synonyms:s:saṅkoca]] [[synonyms:v:vyavahāra]]---dealings with great care.

===== Translation =====

**Everyone knew that Dāmodara Paṇḍita was strict in practical dealings. Therefore everyone was afraid of him and dared not do anything independent.**
