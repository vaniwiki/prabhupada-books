====== Cc. Antya 7.77 ======

===== Text =====

> tabe mahāprabhu sabāra nṛtya rākhilā
> pūrvavat āpane nṛtya karite lāgilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tabe]]---thereafter; [[synonyms:m:mahāprabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:sabāra]]---of all of them; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:r:rākhilā]]---stopped the dancing; [[synonyms:p:pūrvavat]]---as previously; [[synonyms:ā:āpane]]---personally; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]]---dancing; [[synonyms:k:karite]] [[synonyms:l:lāgilā]]---began to perform.

===== Translation =====

**Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stopped the dancing of the others, and as He had done previously, He personally began to dance.**
