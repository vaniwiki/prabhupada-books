====== Cc. Antya 8.95 ======

===== Text =====

> īśvara-caritra prabhura---buddhira agocara
> yabe yei karena, sei saba---manohara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]-[[synonyms:c:caritra]]---character exactly like that of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:b:buddhira]] [[synonyms:a:agocara]]---beyond one's intelligence; [[synonyms:y:yabe]]---when; [[synonyms:y:yei]]---whatever; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---He did; [[synonyms:s:sei]]---that; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:m:manohara]]---very beautiful.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu behaved exactly like the Supreme Personality of Godhead, beyond the restriction of anyone's intelligence. Whatever He liked He did, but all His activities were very beautiful.**
