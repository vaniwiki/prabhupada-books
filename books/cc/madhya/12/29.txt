====== Cc. Madhya 12.29 ======

===== Text =====

> yadyapi īśvara tumi parama svatantra
> tathāpi svabhāve hao prema-paratantra

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:y:yadyapi]]---although; [[synonyms:ī:īśvara]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead; [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---You; [[synonyms:p:parama]]---supremely; [[synonyms:s:svatantra]]---independent; [[synonyms:t:tathāpi]]---still; [[synonyms:s:svabhāve]]---by Your nature; [[synonyms:h:hao]]---You become; [[synonyms:p:prema]]-[[synonyms:p:paratantra]]---subordinate to love.

===== Translation =====

**"Although You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead and are completely independent, still You are dependent on the love and affection of Your devotees. That is Your nature."**
