====== Cc. Madhya 15.246 ======

===== Text =====

> bhojana dekhite cāhe, āsite nā pāre
> lāṭhī-hāte bhaṭṭācārya āchena duyāre

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:b:bhojana]]---the eating; [[synonyms:d:dekhite]] [[synonyms:c:cāhe]]---he wanted to see; [[synonyms:ā:āsite]] [[synonyms:n:nā]] [[synonyms:p:pāre]]---could not come; [[synonyms:l:lāṭhī]]-[[synonyms:h:hāte]]---with a stick in his hand; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭācārya]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:ā:āchena]]---was; [[synonyms:d:duyāre]]---on the threshold.

===== Translation =====

**Amogha wanted to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu eat, but he was not allowed to enter. Indeed, Bhaṭṭācārya guarded the threshold of his house with a stick in his hand.**
