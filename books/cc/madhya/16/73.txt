====== Cc. Madhya 16.73 ======

===== Text =====

> varṣāntare punaḥ tāṅrā aiche praśna kaila
> vaiṣṇavera tāratamya prabhu śikhāila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:v:varṣāntare]]---after one year; [[synonyms:p:punaḥ]]---again; [[synonyms:t:tāṅrā]]---they (the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma); [[synonyms:a:aiche]]---such; [[synonyms:p:praśna]]---a question; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:v:vaiṣṇavera]]---of Vaiṣṇavas; [[synonyms:t:tāratamya]]---upper and lower gradations; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ś:śikhāila]]---taught.

===== Translation =====

**The following year, the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma again asked the Lord the same question. Hearing this question, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again taught them about the different types of Vaiṣṇavas.**
