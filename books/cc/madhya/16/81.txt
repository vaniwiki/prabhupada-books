====== Cc. Madhya 16.81 ======

===== Text =====

> gāla phulila, ācārya antare ullāsa
> vistāri' varṇiyāchena vṛndāvana-dāsa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:gāla]]---the cheeks; [[synonyms:p:phulila]]---became swollen; [[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---Puṇḍarīka Vidyānidhi; [[synonyms:a:antare]]---within the heart; [[synonyms:u:ullāsa]]---very happy; [[synonyms:v:vistāri']]---elaborating; [[synonyms:v:varṇiyāchena]]---has narrated; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]-[[synonyms:d:dāsa]]---Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura.

===== Translation =====

**Although his cheeks were swollen from the slapping, Puṇḍarīka Vidyānidhi was very happy within. This incident has been elaborately described by Ṭhākura Vṛndāvana dāsa.**
