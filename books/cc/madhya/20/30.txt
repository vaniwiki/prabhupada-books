====== Cc. Madhya 20.30 ======

===== Text =====

> tomā māri' mohara la-itāma ājikāra rātrye
> bhāla haila, kahilā tumi, chuṭilāṅa pāpa haite

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tomā]] [[synonyms:m:māri']]---killing you; [[synonyms:m:mohara]]---golden coins; [[synonyms:l:la]]-[[synonyms:i:itāma]]---I would have taken; [[synonyms:ā:ājikāra]] [[synonyms:r:rātrye]]---on this night; [[synonyms:b:bhāla]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---it was very good; [[synonyms:k:kahilā]] [[synonyms:t:tumi]]---you have spoken; [[synonyms:c:chuṭilāṅa]]---I am relieved; [[synonyms:p:pāpa]] [[synonyms:h:haite]]---from such a sin.

===== Translation =====

**"On this very night I would have killed you and taken your coins. It is very good that you have voluntarily offered them to me. I am now relieved from such a sinful activity.**
