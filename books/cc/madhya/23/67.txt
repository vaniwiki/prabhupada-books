====== Cc. Madhya 23.67 ======

===== Text =====

> nāyakānāṁ śiroratnaṁ
> kṛṣṇas tu bhagavān svayam
> yatra nityatayā sarve
> virājante mahā-guṇāḥ

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:n:nāyakānām]]---of all heroes; [[synonyms:ś:śiroratnam]]---the crown jewel; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇaḥ]]---Lord Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:t:tu]]---but; [[synonyms:b:bhagavān]] [[synonyms:s:svayam]]---the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself; [[synonyms:y:yatra]]---in whom; [[synonyms:n:nityatayā]]---with permanence; [[synonyms:s:sarve]]---all; [[synonyms:v:virājante]]---exist; [[synonyms:m:mahā]]-[[synonyms:g:guṇāḥ]]---transcendental qualities.

===== Translation =====

**“ 'Kṛṣṇa is the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself, and He is the crown jewel of all heroes. In Kṛṣṇa, all transcendental good qualities are permanently situated.'**

===== Purport =====

This verse is also found in the //Bhakti-rasāmṛta-sindhu// (2.1.17).
