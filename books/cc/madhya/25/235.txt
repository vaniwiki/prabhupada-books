====== Cc. Madhya 25.235 ======

===== Text =====

> sabā saṅge lañā prabhu miśra-vāsā āilā
> sārvabhauma, paṇḍita-gosāñi nimantraṇa kailā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sabā]] [[synonyms:s:saṅge]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking all of them; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:miśra]]-[[synonyms:v:vāsā]] [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came to Kāśī Miśra's house; [[synonyms:s:sārvabhauma]]---Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya; [[synonyms:p:paṇḍita]]-[[synonyms:g:gosāñi]]---Gadādhara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nimantraṇa]] [[synonyms:k:kailā]]---invited the Lord to take //prasāda.//

===== Translation =====

**The Lord and all His devotees then went to the residence of Kāśī Miśra. Sārvabhauma Bhaṭṭācārya and Paṇḍita Gosāñi also invited the Lord to dine at their homes.**
