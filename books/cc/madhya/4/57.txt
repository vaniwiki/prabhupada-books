====== Cc. Madhya 4.57 ======

===== Text =====

> keha gāya, keha nāce, mahotsava haila
> dadhi, dugdha, ghṛta āila grāme yata chila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:g:gāya]]---some sing; [[synonyms:k:keha]] [[synonyms:n:nāce]]---some dance; [[synonyms:m:mahotsava]] [[synonyms:h:haila]]---there was a festival; [[synonyms:d:dadhi]]---yogurt; [[synonyms:d:dugdha]]---milk; [[synonyms:g:ghṛta]]---clarified butter; [[synonyms:ā:āila]]---was brought; [[synonyms:g:grāme]]---in the village; [[synonyms:y:yata]]---as much; [[synonyms:c:chila]]---as there was.

===== Translation =====

**During the festival at the installation ceremony, some people sang and some danced. All the milk, yogurt and clarified butter in the village was brought to the festival.**
