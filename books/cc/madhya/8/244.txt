====== Cc. Madhya 8.244 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu puche, rāmānanda karena uttara
> ei mata sei rātre kathā paraspara

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhupuche]]---the Lord inquires; [[synonyms:r:rāmānanda]]---Rāya Rāmānanda; [[synonyms:k:karena]]---gives; [[synonyms:u:uttara]]---answers; [[synonyms:e:ei]] [[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:r:rātre]]---on that night; [[synonyms:k:kathā]]---discussion; [[synonyms:p:paraspara]]---mutual.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked the questions, and Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya gave the answers. In this way they were engaged in discussion throughout the night.**
