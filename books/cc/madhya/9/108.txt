====== Cc. Madhya 9.108 ======

===== Text =====

> ei-mata bhaṭṭa-gṛhe rahe gauracandra
> nirantara bhaṭṭa-saṅge kṛṣṇa-kathānanda

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:ei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---in this way; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:g:gṛhe]]---in the house of Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:r:rahe]]---remained; [[synonyms:g:gauracandra]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:n:nirantara]]---constantly; [[synonyms:b:bhaṭṭa]]-[[synonyms:s:saṅge]]---with Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa; [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]-[[synonyms:k:kathā]]-[[synonyms:ā:ānanda]]---the transcendental bliss of talking about Kṛṣṇa.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at the house of Veṅkaṭa Bhaṭṭa and constantly talked with him about Lord Kṛṣṇa. In this way He was very happy.**
