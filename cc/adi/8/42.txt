====== Cc. Ādi 8.42 ======

===== Text =====

> tāṅra ki adbhuta caitanya-carita-varṇana
> yāhāra śravaṇe śuddha kaila tri-bhuvana

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:t:tāṅra]]---Śrīla Vṛndāvana dāsa Ṭhākura's; [[synonyms:k:ki]]---what; [[synonyms:a:adbhuta]]---wonderful; [[synonyms:c:caitanya]]-[[synonyms:c:carita]]---of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:v:varṇana]]---description; [[synonyms:y:yāhāra]]---of which; [[synonyms:ś:śravaṇe]]---by hearing; [[synonyms:ś:śuddha]]---purified; [[synonyms:k:kaila]]---made; [[synonyms:t:tri]]-[[synonyms:b:bhuvana]]---the three worlds.

===== Translation =====

**What a wonderful description he has given of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya! Anyone in the three worlds who hears it is purified.**
