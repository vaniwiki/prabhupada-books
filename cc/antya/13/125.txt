====== Cc. Antya 13.125 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura ṭhāñi ājñā lañā gelā vṛndāvane
> āśraya karilā āsi' rūpa-sanātane

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]] [[synonyms:ṭ:ṭhāñi]]---from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ājñā]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking permission; [[synonyms:g:gelā]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---went to Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:ā:āśraya]] [[synonyms:k:karilā]]---took shelter; [[synonyms:ā:āsi']]---coming; [[synonyms:r:rūpa]]-[[synonyms:s:sanātane]]---of Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī.

===== Translation =====

**Taking permission from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Raghunātha Bhaṭṭa then departed for Vṛndāvana. When he arrived there, he put himself under the care of Rūpa and Sanātana Gosvāmīs.**
