====== Cc. Antya 20.43 ======

===== Text =====

> eteka cintite rādhāra nirmala hṛdaya
> svābhāvika premāra svabhāva karila udaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eteka]]---thus; [[synonyms:c:cintite]]---thinking; [[synonyms:r:rādhāra]]---of Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī; [[synonyms:n:nirmala]] [[synonyms:h:hṛdaya]]---possessing a pure heart; [[synonyms:s:svābhāvika]]---natural; [[synonyms:p:premāra]]---of love of Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sva]]-[[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---the character; [[synonyms:k:karila]] [[synonyms:u:udaya]]---awakes.

===== Translation =====

**While Śrīmatī Rādhārāṇī was thinking in this way, the characteristics of natural love became manifest because of Her pure heart.**
