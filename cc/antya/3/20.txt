====== Cc. Antya 3.20 ======

===== Text =====

> eteka vicāri' prabhu madhyāhne calilā
> āra dine dāmodare nibhṛte bolāilā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:e:eteka]] [[synonyms:v:vicāri']]---considering like this; [[synonyms:p:prabhu]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:m:madhyāhne]] [[synonyms:c:calilā]]---went to perform His noon duties; [[synonyms:ā:āradine]]---the next day; [[synonyms:d:dāmodare]]---unto Dāmodara Paṇḍita; [[synonyms:n:nibhṛte]]---in a solitary place; [[synonyms:b:bolāilā]]---called.

===== Translation =====

**Thinking in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to perform His noon duties. The next day, He called Dāmodara Paṇḍita to a solitary place.**
