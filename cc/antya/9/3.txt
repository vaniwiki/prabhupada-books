====== Cc. Antya 9.3 ======

===== Text =====

> jayādvaitācārya jaya jaya dayāmaya
> jaya gaura-bhakta-gaṇa saba rasamaya

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:a:advaita]]-[[synonyms:ā:ācārya]]---to Advaita Ācārya; [[synonyms:j:jaya]] [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:d:dayā]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---merciful; [[synonyms:j:jaya]]---all glories; [[synonyms:g:gaura]]-[[synonyms:b:bhakta]]-[[synonyms:g:gaṇa]]---to the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:s:saba]]---all; [[synonyms:r:rasa]]-[[synonyms:m:maya]]---overwhelmed by transcendental bliss.

===== Translation =====

**All glories to Advaita Ācārya, who is very merciful! All glories to the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who are always overwhelmed by transcendental bliss!**
