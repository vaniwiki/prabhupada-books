====== Cc. Madhya 1.80 ======

===== Text =====

> sei bhāva, sei kṛṣṇa, sei vṛndāvana
> yabe pāi, tabe haya vāñchita pūraṇa

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:b:bhāva]]---that circumstance; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:k:kṛṣṇa]]---that Kṛṣṇa; [[synonyms:s:sei]] [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvana]]---that Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:y:yabepāi]]---if I get; [[synonyms:t:tabe]]---then; [[synonyms:h:haya]]---is; [[synonyms:v:vāñchita]]---desired object; [[synonyms:p:pūraṇa]]---fulfilled.

===== Translation =====

**Thus meeting with Kṛṣṇa and thinking of the Vṛndāvana atmosphere, Rādhārāṇī longed for Kṛṣṇa to take Her to Vṛndāvana again to fulfill Her desire in that calm atmosphere.**
