====== Cc. Madhya 10.165 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhu kahe,-satya kahi, tomara āgamane
> dui brahma prakaṭila śrī-puruṣottame

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhukahe]]---Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; [[synonyms:s:satya]] [[synonyms:k:kahi]]---I speak the truth; [[synonyms:t:tomāra]] [[synonyms:ā:āgamane]]---by your presence; [[synonyms:d:dui]] [[synonyms:b:brahma]]---two Brahmans; [[synonyms:p:prakaṭila]]---appeared; [[synonyms:ś:śrī]]-[[synonyms:p:puruṣottame]]---at Jagannātha Purī.

===== Translation =====

**Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Actually, to tell you the truth, due to your presence there are now two Brahmans at Jagannātha Purī.**
