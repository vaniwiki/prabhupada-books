====== Cc. Madhya 17.87 ======

===== Text =====

> ghare lañā āilā prabhuke ānandita hañā
> sevā kari' nṛtya kare vastra uḍāñā

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:g:ghare]] [[synonyms:l:lañā]]---taking to his home; [[synonyms:ā:āilā]]---came; [[synonyms:p:prabhuke]]---Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:ā:ānandita]] [[synonyms:h:hañā]]---in great happiness; [[synonyms:s:sevā]] [[synonyms:k:kari']]---rendering service; [[synonyms:n:nṛtya]] [[synonyms:k:kare]]---began to dance; [[synonyms:v:vastra]] [[synonyms:u:uḍāñā]]---waving his cloth.

===== Translation =====

**With great pleasure Tapana Miśra brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home and rendered service unto Him. Indeed, he began to dance, waving his cloth.**
