====== Cc. Madhya 18.56 ======

===== Text =====

> prabhura gamana-rīti pūrve ye likhila
> sei-mata vṛndāvane tāvat dekhila

===== Synonyms =====

[[synonyms:p:prabhura]]---of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; [[synonyms:g:gamana]]-[[synonyms:r:rīti]]---method of touring; [[synonyms:p:pūrve]]---formerly; [[synonyms:y:ye]]---which; [[synonyms:l:likhila]]---I have written; [[synonyms:s:sei]]-[[synonyms:m:mata]]---similarly; [[synonyms:v:vṛndāvane]]---at Vṛndāvana; [[synonyms:t:tāvat]] [[synonyms:d:dekhila]]---saw all the places.

===== Translation =====

**Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's touring Vṛndāvana has been previously described. In the same ecstatic way, He traveled all over Vṛndāvana.**
